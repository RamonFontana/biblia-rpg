# Quickstart: Sistema de Equipamentos

To test the equipment feature once implemented:

1. Log into the application and select a character.
2. Ensure you have items in your inventory (e.g., an armor, a shield, and a 1-handed weapon).
3. Open the Inventory view. You should see "Equip" buttons next to equippable items.
4. Click "Equip" on the armor. Verify that your character's `equipment.body` is updated and CA increases.
5. Equip a 2-handed weapon (e.g., Longbow). Verify that both Main Hand and Off Hand slots are occupied by it.
6. Check your character sheet / combat view to ensure that your damage roll for the Longbow correctly uses `1d8`.
